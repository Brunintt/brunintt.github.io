The podcast was developed for academic purposes by students of first high school year of system development, based in Portuguese Language, Literature and Professional Communication classes.

What is a podcast? Basically it's a radio show that can be listened on the Internet anytime through a cell phone or computer. Through different themes and durations, the listener can access the audio content to obtain information, learn or spend time. Our podcast is based in George Orwell's 1984 book. The selected theme is mass manipulation. We interviewed two teachers of ETEC Parque Belém.
